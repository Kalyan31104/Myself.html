Konidala Pawan Kalyan[2] (born Konidala Kalyan Babu; 2 September 1971[a]) is an Indian politician and actor who has been serving as the 10th deputy chief minister of Andhra Pradesh since June 2024. He is also serving as the Minister of Panchayat Raj, Rural Development & Rural Water Supply; Environment, Forests, Science & Technology in the Government of Andhra Pradesh. He is the founder and president of the Jana Sena Party.

As an actor, Kalyan primarily works in Telugu cinema and is known for his unique style and mannerisms. He has a huge fan base[3] and is one of the highest-paid actors of Indian cinema. He has been featured in Forbes India's Celebrity 100 list multiple times since 2013. Kalyan is the recipient of a Filmfare Award South and a SIIMA Award among other accolades.[7]

Kalyan married Nandini in 1997, one year after his film debut.
